# Welcome to Field Notes

This is your first post in the Field Notes blog. Here, you can share your thoughts, discoveries, and stories in a beautiful, journal-inspired format.

## Getting Started

To publish a new post:

1. Write your content in Google Docs
2. Download it as a .docx file
3. Visit the [Admin page](/admin) to upload and publish

## Features

This blog includes:

- **Beautiful typography** with serif fonts that evoke a classic journal feel
- **Field Notes aesthetic** with warm parchment backgrounds and botanical accents
- **Simple publishing** - just upload your .docx file and you're done
- **Automatic formatting** - your Google Docs content is converted to beautiful Markdown

## Writing Tips

When writing in Google Docs:

- Use headings for structure (Heading 1, Heading 2, etc.)
- Add emphasis with **bold** and *italic* text
- Create lists for easy reading
- Add images directly in your document

Your content will be automatically converted and formatted beautifully on the blog.
